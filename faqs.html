Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is the “YIPLI” smart mat?
Yipli is an interactive fitness mat that records all your activities and gives real-time feedback when you play YIPLI games and practice your workouts. It’s like the regular Yoga mat (6ftx2ft) made of high-quality materials lined with sensors to detect your actions. The materials are eco-friendly and safe for all ages to use.

Q. What ages are Yipli Smart mat designed for?
The Yipli smart mat is designed for use of the entire family starting from 5 years to 75! Even younger children can play some simple games on the mat.

Q. How do I set up my Yipli Smart Mat?
See our helpful step-by-step instructions below:
1.Download the YIPLI APP
2.Signup/Login
3.Scan the Mat



Q Which Devices are compatible with Yipli Smart mat?
“Yipli” is compatible with Mobile Phones (Android & IOS), Tabs, Desktop and Laptops. It can be cast on TV with any casting device like a Chromecast or Apple TV for a richer immersive experience.

Q. Can “YIPLI” be used by more than one person?
Yes. You can have multiple users on one mat. You need to add a player in the “Add Player” section of the app every time a new player uses the mat. Also, your friends and guests can play and enjoy it. It is important that you change the player in the app each time a different user plays on the mat to get accurate tracking of each players fitness analytics.

Q. How do I charge the YIPLI mat?
You can charge the mat via the regular micro USB charger a 4mts long charging/PC connection cable is provided with the kit. A regular charger can charge it to full power in approx. 90 minutes

Q. How long will the YIPLI Smart mat hold the charge?
YIPLI is designed to hold a charge for 6 hours of non-stop practice without requiring a recharge. The battery may discharge if not used for several days. Generally, under regular use, the battery can hold for 1 week without using and can work for about 6 half-an-hour sessions in a week.

Q. Can the design on the Yipli Smart mat be customized?
Yes, the top cover can be customized at some extra cost. Please contact the helpdesk for more details

Q. What happens if my Yipli Smart mat stops working?
Incase if your Yipli mat stops working, feel free to call our support team for further assistance or ping them from the app or the website. Simple troubleshooting steps:
a) If the mat is not connecting with the App, please check if your phone Bluetooth is on. Then pls check the battery level of the mat and if the battery level is low (amber or red light) please charge it for at least 30 mins. If the battery level is ok (green light) then please switch off and switch back on and try again. Please contact the helpdesk if the problem persists
b) In case the mat is not working properly after rolling it and reopening it or prolonged non-usage, please hold the mat with two hands and give it a gentle ripple (like you would do with your bedsheet to dust it). Restart the mat and the App and try again. Please contact the helpdesk if the problem persists

Q. Can I wash my YIPLI mat?
No. Though the mat is made up of water-resistant materials washing or excessive water might spoil the electronics of the mat. You can however wipe with a wet cloth gently or you may use a light non-abrasive spray to clean the surface.
