How adult.fit Personal Training works

  • STEP 1 : Find your ideal Training Journey

  • Fill out your profile to find the path toward your personal goal with the Yipli Coach.

  • STEP 2 : Free up yourself from excuses

  • You decide where, when and how to train. No equipment. No classes to sign up for. No excuses.

  • STEP 3 : Get the right guidance

  • Train at the right level, with the right exercises, for the results you want. Stay on track. Always.

  • STEP 4 : Workout with your companion

  • Your personal YIPLI companion will track your performance, motivate you to do more, cheer up for your achievements and fuel your competitive side!

  • The Coach learns from your training feedback and your performance this week affects next week’s workouts.

  • Share results, get motivated and meet and beat your buddies in friendly challenges. Together, we are stronger, together we will get fiker.
